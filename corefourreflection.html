Conditionals

Definition: executes actions based on whether a boolean statement is true or false.
ex) today = Saturday
if it is Saturday or Sunday:
it is the weekend.

real life analogy: If I have $10 and a movie ticket is $9, then I can buy a movie ticket.

Loops

Definition: sequence of instructions that is continually repeated until a certain condition is reached.
ex) i=0
for i in range(sides):
forward(100)
